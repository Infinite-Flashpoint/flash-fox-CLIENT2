FAQ

Is it free ?

Uploading your files is totally free. You can upload files without login, go and share files that you think interesting to others

What can I upload ?

You can upload any file as long as they are legal, once again, LEGAL. We are very strict about laws so any uploaded file that violates any law or our terms of use will be deleted immediately, but well you can still upload that files, so please don't do that

How do I share ?

It is really easy to share your files. After you complete an upload, a download link will be generated. You can share this link with anyone via facebook, twitter or email.
